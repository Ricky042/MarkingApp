# Welcome to MarkingApp

Welcome to **MarkingApp**, a web-based tool designed to help educators plan courses, track tutor progress, and manage grading efficiently — all in one place.

Whether you’re organizing a new semester, uploading assessment results, or providing feedback to markers, this guide will walk you through everything you need to know.

---

## What You Can Do with MarkingApp

- **Plan and organize courses** with drag-and-drop tools
- **Grade students efficiently** using automated averages and exportable reports
- **Upload and manage files** across courses
- **Manage your account settings** and preferences
