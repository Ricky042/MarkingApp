# Getting Started

This guide will help you log in, navigate the dashboard, and get ready to use MarkingApp.

---

## 1. Log In

Visit [markingapp-frontend.onrender.com/login](#) and enter your email and password.  
If you don’t have an account yet, press the signup button and create your own login details, make sure to verify your email!

**Tip:** Bookmark the login page for quick access next time.

---

## 2. Creating your first team

If you don't have one already or haven't been invited to one, you will be prompted to create a team upon logging in. If you're attempting to create a new team, navigate to the settings page and press on the create team button.

Teams keep assignments seperate and allow other users to create their own teams for their own subjects.

**Tip:** Name your team something memorable!

---

## 3. Explore the Dashboard

Once logged in, you’ll see your **Dashboard** — the main control panel for your work.
This page is seperate for each of your teams / classes.

You’ll find:
- **Dashboard Details:** Shows you some quick details about your teams assignments.
- **Calendar:** Shows upcoming deadlines and dates.
- **Quick Actions:** Invite markers and show reports for assignments.
- **Assignments:** Manage and mark current assignments.
- **Markers:** View your teams current members and their details.
- **Reports / Exports:** View more advanced details about assignments and their statistics.
- **Settings:** Change user settings, create teams, switch between teams.

---

## 4. Create Your First Assignment (For Team Owners)

1. Click **+ New Assignment** on the dashboard.  
2. Fill in details such as course name, semester, and add different team members.
3. Add / Fill in the rubric tempate and adjust criterion and mark assignment.
4. Upload the students control paper which your tutors will be marking
5. Review details 
6. Press **Save** to confirm.

Your new assignment will appear in the list and be accessible from the **Assignments Page**.
